🌍 The Philosophy

Free. Private.
For Everyone.

Sweeask is not just a task tracker. It is a statement — that powerful productivity tools should belong to everyone, not just those who can afford a subscription.

⭐ Star on GitHub Open App →
⚙️ Foundation

Built on four pillars

These principles guide every decision we make — from what features we build to how data is stored.

Pillar 01
🔐

Privacy by Design

Your tasks, habits, and schedules are deeply personal. Sweeask stores everything locally on your device using localStorage. We have no servers, no databases, no accounts. We cannot read your data because we never receive it. A data breach is mathematically impossible for us because there is nothing to breach.

No Login Required No Tracking No Cloud Sync
Pillar 02
🌐

Universal Accessibility

A professional productivity tool should not require expensive hardware or a paid subscription. Sweeask runs on every browser, on every device — from a flagship laptop to a low-end phone. One codebase. One experience. Available as a Web App, installable PWA, and offline HTML file. Zero barriers to entry.

Works on Any Browser Offline First
Pillar 03
📊

Visual Intelligence

Humans are visual creatures. A list of text is not motivating — a heatmap of your consistency is. A streak counter, a progress ring, a 30-day trend line: these are the elements that turn task management into a personal narrative of growth. We believe the interface you work in directly affects the quality of the work you produce.

Heatmaps Charts & Analytics Streak Tracking
Pillar 04
🔧

Democratization of Power Tools

Notion, Todoist, and Jira are powerful — but they cost money, require accounts, and store your data in the cloud. Sweeask brings the same capabilities — recurring tasks, kanban, focus timers, analytics — to anyone with a browser. No price tag. No compromise. The productivity forge, open to all.

Free Forever Open Source

🗺️ Roadmap

Where we are going

An honest look at what's shipped, what's in progress, and what's coming next.

v1.0.0
Shipped

Core Task Tracker

Basic task list with priorities, types, dates, and a weekly chart. Offline localStorage. CSV export.

v1.1.0
Shipped

Recurring Tasks + Time Ranges

Daily, weekday, weekly, monthly recurrence. Start/end times. Today view grouped by time of day. Separate completion tracking per date.

v1.2.0
In Progress

Sweeask Website + PWA

Full public website with landing page, about, support, updates pages. PWA install, service worker, manifest. Multi-theme support.

v1.3.0
Planned

Command Palette (Ctrl+K)

A VS Code-style command palette for instant navigation, task creation, and theme switching without touching the mouse.

v1.4.0
Planned

Local AI "Jimmy" — NLP Task Creation

Type naturally: "Review code tomorrow at 3pm, high priority" and the AI parses date, time, and priority automatically. Runs fully offline via Transformers.js.

v2.0.0
Future

P2P Collaboration + CRDT Sync

Serverless task sharing via WebRTC. Two users sync tasks directly, browser-to-browser, with no cloud database. Conflict-free merging via Yjs CRDTs.
